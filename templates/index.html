About

This application helps doctors to predict the fetal heath of a patient and also detects any risks of cervical cancer in just the click of a button. It also helps to determine the correlation of a particular feature with other features so that doctors will be able to comprehend the importance and dependency of that feature with other features. The application provides a nutrition guide to women as well so that there wouldn't be any case of anomalies due to malnutrition.
